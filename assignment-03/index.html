Chicken Stir Fry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper
  • 1/2 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup baby carrots
  • 2 tsp minced ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 2 tbsp cold water
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together corn starch and water Add remaining
    ingredients (chicken broth, soy sauce, honey, toasted sesame oil, and red pepper
    flakes) and whisk to combine. Set aside.
  2. Add 1 tbsp of olive oil to a large skillet or wok and heat over medium high
    heat.
  3. Add chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook for
    3-5 minutes or until cooked through. Remove from skillet.
  4. Reduce heat to medium and add remaining tbsp of oil to the skillet.
  5. Add broccoli, bell pepper, and carrots and cook, stirring occasionally, just until crisp
    tender. Add ginger and garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  6. Add chicken back into skillet and stir to combine.
  7. Whisk stir fry sauce and pour over chicken and vegetables and stir gently to
    combine.
  8. Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally, and let boil for 1 minute.
  9. Serve with rice and/or chow mein if desired.
